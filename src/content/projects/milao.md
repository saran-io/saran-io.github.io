## Overview

Milao is an AI-powered communication skills trainer built for students and professionals who want to speak with clarity, confidence, and impact. The platform combines speech intelligence, real-time feedback, and structured learning workflows to help users measurably improve over time.

## My Role — Chief Product Engineer

I lead the product and engineering side of Milao, owning the full stack from AI architecture to the user-facing product experience.

**What I work on:**
- Designing and building the AI and ML systems that power speech analysis
- Speech intelligence pipelines — accent, fluency, pacing, filler words, clarity
- Personalized feedback engine that adapts to each learner's progression
- Product architecture and platform infrastructure
- Bridging the gap between research and a product people actually use

## The problem

Most communication training is either expensive (1-on-1 coaches), generic (YouTube videos), or unmeasurable (self-practice). Milao closes the gap — instant, intelligent feedback every time you speak, with a learning path that actually moves you forward.

## Stack

- Speech recognition and NLP for real-time analysis
- Custom ML models for communication quality scoring
- AI feedback engine with personalized learning loops
- Scalable product platform built for learners at every level
