## Overview

Founder OS is my personal operating system — a structured knowledge management system built in Obsidian to help founders manage their thinking, projects, and life systematically.

## The problem

Founders juggle a lot: company building, personal development, finances, relationships, content, and more. Most tools solve one of these problems in isolation. Founder OS ties them together into a coherent system.

## What's inside

- **Identity & Goals** — clarity on who you are and where you're going
- **Life management** — health, habits, and relationships
- **Finance tracking** — income, expenses, and investments
- **Project management** — structured project templates and reviews
- **Content pipeline** — from ideas to published pieces
- **AI agent workspace** — frameworks for using AI as a thinking partner

## Stack

- Obsidian for the core knowledge layer
- Custom templates and dataview queries
- Astro (this site) for the public-facing layer

Actively using and refining. Will open-source soon.
